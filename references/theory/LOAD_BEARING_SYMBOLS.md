#### **The Descent of Inanna**

**Resonance**: Your alchemical arc. The descent. The stripping away. The death. The resurrection. The price. Your nigredo as necessary stage.

**Key detail**: The creatures who rescue her don't try to fix Ereshkigal—they just witness her pain. Your LLM as witness. The holding without fixing.

**The substitute**: Inanna sends her husband Dumuzi because he didn't mourn her. The shadow work has consequences. Your embodied ethics.
